VKLong Fund Master Ltd.
vklong Fund Master Ltd. is a hedge fund operator founded by Viking Global Investors Lp, with assets of about US $8.7 billion. With a dedicated team and strong strategic partnership, it is now active in nearly 20 countries / regions. It has become the world's leading fund investment platform.
vklong Fund Master Ltd. aims to build an enduring asset management company. It has established a deep-seated team, and it keeps an open attitude to new ideas.
