VKLong Fund Master Ltd.
vklong Fund Master Ltd. is a hedge fund operator founded by Viking Global Investors Lp, with assets of about US $8.7 billion. With a dedicated team and strong strategic partnership, it is now active in nearly 20 countries / regions. It has become the world's leading fund investment platform.
vklong Fund Master Ltd. aims to build an enduring asset management company. It has established a deep-seated team, and it keeps an open attitude to new ideas.
About VKLong Planning
In order to explore and study the investment market other than stock funds, vklong Fund Master Ltd. created HopingClub, which brings together high-end talents from various industries and brings a broader investment vision and more precipitation of cross industry investment experience. At present, HopingClub will evaluate according to the past market situation and expected performance of the market, explore the quality of fund management and complementary fund strategies, and invest in assets with excellent potential in many fields. It is committed to developing an ecological investment and financial management plan.
